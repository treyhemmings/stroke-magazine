stroke magazine
founded in 2020 as an artist-run dialogue on contemporary art, stroke magazine has evolved into a digital publication led by editor-in-chief yioryios papayioryiou and creative director trey hemmings. stroke was pioneered to connect artists from around the world to cultivate a global community of contemporary art that fosters collaboration and visibility across a diverse variety of creative mediums.
